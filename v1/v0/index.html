Our flavors

We offer 3 ways of implementing our client SDK:

Complete checkout flow tool: by adding just a piece of code and a few configurations, get a full checkout flow running in your app.

Data collection UI: Use our SDK's provided UI to simplify data collection, and then make payments by yourself.

Customizable UI: complete customizable checkout flow using MercadoPago SDK's methods to retrieve data.
