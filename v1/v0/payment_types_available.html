Payments Types and Payment Methods available

Payment types and methods will differ according the public key of your country. You can use this payment info to exclude payment types and methods in a preference and in the checkout flow or in the payment method selection component.

Payment Types

Payment types available and ID's:

  • Credit card: "credit_card"
  • Debit card: "debit_card"
  • Prepaid card: "prepaid_card"
  • Account money: "account_money"
  • Ticket: "ticket"
  • Bank transfer: "bank_transfer"
  • Atm: "atm"
  • Digital currency: "digital_currency"

Payment Methods Available

Chose your country to see the available payment methods:

       Argentina
       Brazil
       Colombia
       Chile
       Mexico
       Venezuela

You can exclude payment methods in the checkout flow, specifying those payment method ids in the excludedPaymentMethods list atribute when you can create a CheckoutPreference.
